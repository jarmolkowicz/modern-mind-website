How It Works

The knowledge base lives on GitHub — browse it, contribute to it, build on it. The knowledge pack is one downloadable file you give to your AI.

1. Download

One file. The full knowledge pack bundled into a single markdown, updated with every new entry.

2. Give it to your AI

Drop it into Claude Projects, NotebookLM, a Custom GPT, or any AI assistant that lets you upload files.

3. Prompt

Design, write, audit, or teach — with AI grounded in research instead of generic knowledge.

How It's Built

Evidence-first

Every concept ties back to research, documented practice, or professional insight. Status-tagged so you know the confidence level.

Readable by you and your AI

Consistent formatting, metadata, and cross-references across every entry.

Open source

MIT licensed. Use it, fork it, build on it. Knowledge about how AI affects thinking shouldn't be locked behind a paywall.

Why Open Source

Emerging field

Needs diverse perspectives — practitioners, educators, and researchers who validate and contribute.

Urgent issue

AI capabilities advancing faster than our wisdom about using them.

Too many frameworks

Instead of adding to the noise, building one shared source of truth.

Knowledge should be open

Not locked behind paywalls or proprietary training programs.
